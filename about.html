About


Home History is an education resource designed for use in home schooling and education. It provides learners and educators with the tools required to research and understand historical events and concepts for themselves. The subject content deals specifically with New Zealand history, however the approach to learning taught can be applied to various fields of research.

*Note: Home History is optimised for and best viewed in widescreen aspect ratio. Mobile devices are not recommended. If help is required in getting set up with hardware, please reach out via the Contact page.

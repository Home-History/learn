1. Haere Mai


Welcome to this study of the history of Aotearoa/New Zealand. Throughout this presentation we will learn about some of the key events, people and movements that came to form the nation that we live in today. The purpose of this resource is not only to inform us of our history, but to understand how it is we know history, how history is made, and how it can be changed. Beyond this, we can begin to understand how history shapes the future.

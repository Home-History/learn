Research Task


In this module you were introduced to some origin stories for the Polynesians who peopled New Zealand. These examples should also encourage you to think about the origins of historical narratives themselves.

  • Think about the sources of these origin stories and their purpose. What are they and what do they say about the people they describe?

  • Who or where do the sources originate from? In what way may the narratives told benefit, or be of detriment to whom they are subject?

  • Are there any competing theories to the narratives presented? If so, which do you think are more likely to be true, and why?

    • Spend some time to do some research to obtain any extra required information that may answer your questions. You are encouraged to enter your answers in the text box below so we may return to them at later.
